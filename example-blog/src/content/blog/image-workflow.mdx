## Why both `heroImage` and `coverAssetPath`?

`heroImage` is defined with `image()` in `content.config.ts`.

`coverAssetPath` is just a `string`, but the editor can still treat it as an image field through overrides.

This is useful when your runtime schema must stay generic.
